🚀 HOW TO USE

1. INSTALL
   npm install

   npx react-native run-ios        # iOS

3. TEST
   • Capture or upload a plant image
   • Tap "Analyze Image"
   • View results in table
   • Tap "Zoom & Explore" to pinch-zoom (1-4x)
   • Drag to pan
   • Pinch out to zoom back
